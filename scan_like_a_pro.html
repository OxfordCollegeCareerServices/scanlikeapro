How to scan using your phone 4 simple steps

From paper to PDF in minutes

No scanner needed. Your phone already knows how to do this.

  • 1 Place the paper on a clean flat table or desk.
  • 2 Open Notes, Google Drive, Adobe Scan, Microsoft Lens, or another scan app.
  • 3 Choose Scan Document, capture the page, then save as PDF.
  • 4 For many pages, use Add Page so everything stays in one PDF file.
Make it look professional Visual quality

Small details make your documents look polished and workplace ready.

  • • Use good lighting and smooth the paper.

Name your files clearly File hygiene

Use smart names they can search

Clear file names keep your documents easy to find and hard to lose.

Use names like
FirstName LastName Resume FirstName LastName Consent Form CompanyName FirstName LastName Reference Form

Avoid file names like IMG1234 or Screenshot.

Send documents like a professional

How you share files is part of how people read your application, forms, and requests.

Do this
  • ✓ Send one PDF instead of many separate photos.
  • ✓ Attach the PDF directly to your email.
  • ✓ Use a clear subject line such as Resume for FirstName LastName.
Avoid this
  • ✕ Photos with your bed, legs, feet, or clutter in the background.
  • ✕ Dark, blurry, or cut off pages.
  • ✕ Sharing documents through random links or social media.
